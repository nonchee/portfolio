Meet Pennjamin, the PennApps Penguin. I made Pennjamin during a thirty-day free trial of Adobe Illustrator, and everyone on the PennApps design team got so carried away branding everything with penguins that he ended up with his own Facebook page.

Pennjamin probably went through four or five iterations of various roundness, eye size, and beak style. Developing him was a lot fun as I paid attention to critique from older designers on the team. Towards the end though, they mostly just started going "it's so cuuuuuute".
